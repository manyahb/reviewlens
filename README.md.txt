# ReviewLens

ReviewLens is a Chrome extension that estimates whether e-commerce product reviews are AI-generated.

## Problem

Online shopping reviews are increasingly AI-generated or AI-assisted, making it difficult for users to judge their authenticity.

## Solution

ReviewLens analyzes product reviews and estimates the likelihood that they were generated using AI.

## Version 1 Goals

- Read reviews from Myntra
- Analyze AI likelihood
- Display a confidence score
- Explain the prediction
